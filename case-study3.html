Client Reporting Experience

Creating a clear, client-friendly reporting experience for digital out-of-home campaigns.

Context

Clients received performance reports but often relied on account managers to interpret the results. Reports needed to be clearer, more visual, and easier to navigate.

The Problem

Reports were too technical for non-specialist users and lacked narrative structure, making it difficult for clients to understand campaign success.

My Role

  • Client journey mapping
  • UX writing & content hierarchy
  • Wireframing report layouts
  • Collaboration with data teams

Process

I restructured reports to follow a story: campaign goals, performance summary, and supporting data. Visual elements were introduced to reduce cognitive load and guide interpretation.

Key Screens

Campaign summary
Visual performance charts
Regional breakdown

Impact & Learnings

Clients gained confidence in understanding their results, while internal teams spent less time explaining reports. This reinforced the value of UX writing and narrative-driven design.
