Enterprise Reporting Dashboard

Designing data-heavy dashboards that turn campaign performance into clear, actionable insights.

Context

Internal teams and clients required access to performance data across multiple campaigns, regions, and formats. Existing reports were static and difficult to interpret.

The Problem

Users struggled to understand campaign performance quickly. Metrics lacked hierarchy, and reports required manual explanation from internal teams.

My Role

  • UX & data exploration
  • Information hierarchy & layout design
  • Wireframing dashboards
  • Design system alignment

Process

I worked closely with stakeholders to identify the most critical metrics users needed at a glance. Data was grouped by intent, not volume, and visual hierarchy was used to guide attention.

Key Screens

Overview dashboard
Campaign breakdown
Performance trends

Impact & Learnings

The redesigned dashboard reduced the need for manual explanations and empowered users to interpret performance independently. This project strengthened my approach to data clarity and UX for non-technical users.
