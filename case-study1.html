Digital OOH Management System

Designing an internal platform for managing digital out-of-home inventory, bookings, and reporting at scale.

Context

Blue Label Media operates a large digital out-of-home (DOOH) network across multiple provinces, with inventory spanning high-traffic locations and diverse audience segments.

Internal teams — sales, operations, and reporting — relied on fragmented tools to manage bookings, inventory availability, and campaign performance. As the network scaled, these workflows became increasingly inefficient.

The Problem

The lack of a unified system resulted in duplicated work, manual errors, and limited visibility across teams.

From a UX perspective, users struggled to understand inventory status, campaign progress, and performance metrics quickly — especially under time pressure.

Constraints

  • Multiple user roles with different permissions and goals
  • Legacy processes already embedded in the business
  • Large volumes of data requiring clear hierarchy
  • Limited development resources during early phases

My Role

  • UX research & stakeholder interviews
  • Information architecture
  • Wireframing & interaction design
  • UI design & frontend collaboration

Users & Roles

  • Sales team: Needed fast visibility into available inventory and bookings
  • Operations team: Required accurate scheduling and campaign tracking
  • Management: Needed high-level performance insights
  • Clients: Required clear, easy-to-understand reporting

Process

I began by mapping existing workflows to identify friction points and duplicated effort across teams.

From there, I prioritised core user journeys and designed information architecture that supported both speed and clarity. Wireframes focused on reducing cognitive load while maintaining flexibility for future features.

Key Design Decisions

  • Dashboard-first approach to surface critical information immediately
  • Consistent layout patterns to reduce learning time
  • Clear visual hierarchy for data-heavy views
  • Scalable components aligned to a growing design system

Key Screens

Inventory overview — prioritising availability and status clarity
Dashboard overview
Inventory management
Campaign reporting

Impact

The platform improved cross-team visibility, reduced reliance on spreadsheets, and created a shared source of truth.

Internal teams reported improved efficiency, while clients experienced clearer communication and reporting.

Reflection

This project strengthened my ability to design for complex systems and multiple stakeholders. It reinforced the importance of clarity, collaboration, and designing with future scalability in mind.
